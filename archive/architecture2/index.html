About Euler's Identity

Euler's identity is often cited as an example of mathematical beauty. It elegantly combines five of the most important mathematical constants: e (Euler's number), i (the imaginary unit), π (pi), 1, and 0, along with the three basic arithmetic operations: addition, multiplication, and exponentiation.
